Bring your **passport** and **codice fiscale** (tax code). Some stores may accept passport only for tourists.

End of month often has special promotions. Avoid busy lunch hours (**12:30-15:00**).

Learn key phrases: "Vorrei una SIM card" (I would like a SIM card), "Quanto costa?" (How much does it cost?)
