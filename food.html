Shopping Like a Local: Tips & Key Terms

Navigate the Italian grocery store with confidence.

The Fruit & Veg Scale

Before heading to the checkout, you must weigh your own produce. Put your items in a bag, find the correct code number on the sign, and use the self-service scale to print a barcode sticker to attach to your bag.

The Deli Counter (Gastronomia)

Don't miss the deli counter! It's the best place to get fresh-sliced prosciutto, salami, and local cheeses. Simply take a number, wait for your turn, and tell the clerk what you want and how much (e.g., "un etto di prosciutto cotto" for 100g).
