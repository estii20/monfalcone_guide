Transport Tips

Essential information for getting around

Bus Tickets

Buy tickets at **tobacco shops (`Tabacchi`)**, newsstands, or use the TPL FVG mobile app. **Validate** your ticket immediately upon boarding.

Peak Hours

Avoid 7:00–9:00 AM and 5:00–7:00 PM for less crowded journeys, especially near the shipyards or major industrial areas.

Mobile Apps

Download the **Trenitalia** and **TPL FVG** apps for real-time schedules and digital ticket purchasing.

Monthly Passes

Save money with monthly passes if you travel regularly. They cover both local and regional TPL FVG buses.

Night Services

Public night bus services are very limited. Plan late travel using local taxi services instead.

Accessibility

Most modern TPL FVG buses and Trenitalia trains are wheelchair accessible. Check station information for ramp availability.
