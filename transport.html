Transport Tips

Essential information for getting around

Bus Tickets

Buy tickets at tobacco shops, newsstands, or use the mobile app. Validate before boarding.

Peak Hours

Avoid 7-9 AM and 5-7 PM for less crowded journeys. Plan extra time during rush hours.

Mobile Apps

Download Trenitalia and APT Gorizia apps for real-time schedules and digital tickets.

Monthly Passes

Save money with monthly bus passes if you travel regularly. Available at the main bus station.

Night Services

Limited night bus services available. Check schedules or use taxi services for late travel.

Accessibility

Most buses and trains are wheelchair accessible. Look for accessibility symbols on schedules.
